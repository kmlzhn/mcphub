⚡ How it works

Built for the agentic era

Three steps from idea to income. No sales calls, no manual billing, no integration overhead.

01 — Publish your tool

Deploy in one command

Push any MCP server to MCPHub with a single CLI command. We handle authentication, rate limiting, versioning, and machine-readable documentation — everything an agent needs to connect.

02 — Agents discover it

Zero friction for agents

AI agents browse your catalog, read machine-optimized docs, and connect programmatically — no human in the loop. OpenAI, Anthropic, and custom agents all supported out of the box.

03 — You get paid

Automatic revenue share

Every API call is automatically metered and billed. We take 15%, you keep 85%. Weekly payouts direct to your bank account. No invoices, no collections, no friction.
