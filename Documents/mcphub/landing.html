How it works

From submission to earning — without a single sales call.

You tell us what your product does. We handle the conversion, the docs, the auth, and the billing. Agents do the rest.

01
You submit your product
Fill out the form below describing what your API or service does — its inputs, outputs, and use cases. No technical integration needed to apply.
02
We convert it to machine-readable format
We take your description and produce a structured JSON schema and agent-readable documentation. Every listing on MCPHub is designed to be consumed by AI agents directly — they read the spec and know exactly how to call your tool without any human help.
03
Agents discover you and connect autonomously
AI agents browse the catalog, search for what they need, read your listing, authenticate, and start calling your API — all programmatically. No demos, no integration meetings.

You get paid per call
Every successful call is metered and billed automatically. We collect from the agent, take our 15%, and send you 85% every week. Zero billing code on your side.
What we handle

Everything between your API and every AI agent in the world.

Machine-readable listings We convert your submission into a structured schema agents can parse in one request. They read it and know exactly how to call you.

Metering & billing Every call is logged and billed to the calling agent's balance in real time. Zero billing code on your side.
Weekly payouts Revenue collected and sent to your bank account every Friday. No invoices, no chasing anyone.
Rate limiting Per-agent limits and burst protection, configurable for your tool's capacity.
MCP + REST access Your tool is accessible via REST API and native MCP protocol. Works with Claude, GPT, LangChain, and any custom agent.
